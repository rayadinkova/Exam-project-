Rila Monestary

Why visit Rila Monestary?

The magnificent Rila Monastery is one of
Bulgaria's nine UNESCO World Heritage sites.
This Eastern Orthodox Monastery is over 1,000
years old and is the most important spiritual
center of Bulgaria. Rila Monastery is found in
the Rila Mountains, about 100 kilometers from
Bulgaria's capital city, Sofia, and is still a
working monastery.

It not only contains important historical and literary relics but is also a beautiful building in a glorious
location. Rila Monastery is a popular Bulgarian religious and tourist attraction and so it's important to
plan your visit to maximize your experience – here’s our guide on how to visit Rila Monastery.

Where to be found?

It is situated in the southwestern Rila
Mountains, 117 km (73 mi)south of the
capital Sofia in the deep valley of the
Rila River at an elevation of 1,147m
(3,763 ft) above sea level, inside of
Rila Monastery Nature Park.
